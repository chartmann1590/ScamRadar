PLAY STORE DATA SAFETY FORM

The Data Safety form below mirrors what ScamRadar actually does. Use these
exact answers when filling out the Play Console form.

DOES YOUR APP COLLECT OR SHARE ANY USER DATA?
  Yes — anonymous analytics only.

DOES YOUR APP USE ENCRYPTION IN TRANSIT?
  Yes (Firebase + AdMob use HTTPS).

DO YOU PROVIDE A WAY FOR USERS TO REQUEST DATA DELETION?
  Yes — users can clear scan history from Settings, and uninstalling the
  app removes all on-device data. No server-side data is associated with
  the user.

DATA TYPES COLLECTED:

  App activity → App interactions (anonymous analytics events)
    Collected: Yes
    Shared:    No
    Optional:  Yes (Firebase Analytics can be disabled at OS level)
    Purpose:   Analytics, App functionality

  Device or other IDs → Optional / required for ad serving (AdMob)
    Collected: Yes (advertising ID)
    Shared:    Yes (with Google/AdMob)
    Optional:  Yes (user can opt-out via Android Settings → Ads)
    Purpose:   Advertising or marketing

DATA TYPES *NOT* COLLECTED:

  - Personal info (name, email, address, phone number)
  - Messages or message content (scan input)
  - Photos or videos
  - Audio files (voicemail recordings)
  - Files and documents
  - Contacts
  - Calendar
  - Location
  - Financial info
  - Health & fitness
  - Web browsing history
  - SMS messages

KEY GUARANTEE TO SURFACE IN YOUR LISTING

  "Your messages, screenshots, and voicemails are processed entirely on your
   device. They are never uploaded, never logged, and never seen by us."
